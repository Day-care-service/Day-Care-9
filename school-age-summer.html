School-Age & Summer

We understand that your family’s need a safe, supervised care extends well into your child’s elementary school years. Kids & care Inc. provides an interesting, educational and fun recreation program, supervised by qualified adults. Students enjoy a balance of planned activities (arts and crafts, sports, and games, group projects) and time to unwind (indoor/outdoor play, reading, drawing). When our school ager attends for a full day, field trips and special events add to the fun.

Before/After School Care – Providing the supervision and structure that school agers need and the fun and flexibility they enjoy. Quiet time is also available for homework. As an added convenience, transportation is provided to and from most local elementary schools.

Learning should not stop when school is out for the summer. At Kids & Care Preschool & Daycare Center we work to ensure your child has an educational and fun summer through our curriculum and summer program. We use games, field trips and fun activities to teach your child and to reinforce what they have already learned in school.

Pre-registration is required with limited spots for boys and girls ages 2nd grade and up. We provide a unique and exciting learning experience through various activities:

  • Arts and crafts
  • Indoor and outdoor games
  • Scavenger hunt
  • Sports
  • Field trips
  • Nature hikes
  • Educational enhancement projects
  • Music and song writing
  • Cooking projects
  • And much more!

Summer camp gives kids an opportunity to explore, create, investigate and design as they develop existing skills and learn new ones but most of all create new memories and strengthen friendships. We invite your children to be a part of our summer program, enroll soon as space is limited.
